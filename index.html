Synthoria
San Francisco, California

Computational pipelines for whole-genome sequencing interpretation and population-scale genomic inference.

We develop methods for extracting biological signal from short-read and long-read whole-genome data. Our work spans variant discovery, structural variation, haplotype phasing, and genotype-phenotype analysis across diverse sequencing platforms.

Research Focus

Variant Calling

SNV, indel, and structural variant detection from matched short-read and long-read WGS

Phasing & Assembly

Haplotype-resolved analysis and diploid assembly from HiFi sequencing

Population Genetics

Ancestry inference, admixture, and population structure from WGS cohorts

Annotation & Interpretation

Functional effect prediction, gene-level burden, and multi-source evidence integration

Platforms & Methods
  • Illumina NovaSeq X Plus (PCR-free)
  • PacBio Revio HiFi
  • DeepVariant / DRAGEN benchmarking
  • Long-read SV callers (pbsv, Sniffles2)
  • Pharmacogenomic star-allele calling
  • Methylation from native HiFi kinetics
  • Repeat expansion detection
  • Admixture and PCA (AADR, plink2)

Open to collaborations with sequencing providers, research groups, and platform developers.
